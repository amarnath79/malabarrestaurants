    About Us

    Malabar Plaza Group is a hospitality chain, headquartered in the city of Kochi, the queen of Arabian sea and spread across Kerala also famously known as God’s Own Country. We have varied business interests in Hotels, Restaurants and Retailing. Our Hotels are in the heart of the city with all modern amenities and our Restaurants serve some of the most delicious recipes. We also have a unique series of retail outlets called “The Mask”, with stores and franchises across Kerala.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to be the best-in-class in every new line of business , which we venture into. We aim to be the epitome of quality , authenticity and integrity, true to the ethos and culture of God's Own Country.
